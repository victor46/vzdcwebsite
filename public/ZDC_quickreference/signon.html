VATSIM VIRTUAL WASHINGTON DC ARTCC -- ATC POSITION SIGN-ON CHECKLIST

  • confer with Events Coordinator (EC), Controller In-Charge (CIC), or overlying controller to determine the position you will be working.
  • advise the controller you will be relieving (either equivalent or overlying position) that you are signing on (NOTE: do not use ATC text chat for this).
  • start ATC client and connect with appropriate callsign.
  • edit controller info in ATISmaker, if applicable (must include feedback link: http://vzdc.org/feedback/new).
  • connect Audio For VATSIM and monitor the frequency controlling the position you will be working (NOTE: do not "prime" nor transmit on frequency at this stage).
  • obtain a briefing from the relevant controller to include:
    • field ops direction;
    • general wind / cloud / vis conditions;
    • current ATIS letter (and coordinate who will host ATIS going forward);
    • status of each aircraft;
    • status of any traffic metering in effect;
    • any other issues affecting operation.
  • ask and / or answer any questions to / from the controller being relieved.
  • formal handoff of control: "my control {oper initials} / your control {oper initials}" (NOTE: "prime" and begin using your frequency).
  • TRACON / CTR only: annouce position opening via ATC text chat.
  • REMINDER: controllers working DEL / GND positions may post ATIS at the direction of the controller handling TWR duties; or, must edit text to indicate "suggested" approaches / runways (rather than "in use").

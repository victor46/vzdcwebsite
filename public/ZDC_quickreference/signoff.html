VATSIM VIRTUAL WASHINGTON DC ARTCC -- ATC POSITION SIGN-OFF CHECKLIST

  • 15-minute warning (if the position will not be relieved by a new controller on the same frequency):
    • via ".break" command;
    • on the frequency;
    • to surrounding ATC positions;
    • [TRACON / Center only] via ATC text chat.
  • provide a briefing to the controller assuming duties of your position to include:
    • field ops direction;
    • general wind / cloud / vis conditions;
    • current ATIS letter (and coordinate who will host ATIS going forward);
    • status of each aircraft;
    • status of any traffic metering in effect;
    • any other issues affecting operation.
  • ask and / or answer any questions to / from the controller taking over the position.
  • formal handoff of control: "my control {oper initials} / your control {oper initials}."
  • if position is closing or duties moving to another frequency, announce on frequency and advise where to recieve further services.
  • if TRACON or Enroute position is closing or duties moving to another controller, announce on ATC text chat.
  • disconnect ATIS (if applicable), AFV, and ATC client.
